# North Carolina Driver's License (Durham area)

!!! warning "Bring originals"
    The DMV is strict about identity and residency **originals**. Photocopies are usually not accepted.

## Before you go
- **Proof of identity** (passport, immigration docs)
- **Social Security** proof (card or ineligibility letter)
- **Two proofs of NC residency** (bank statement, lease, utility bill)
- **Insurance** if you plan to register a car
- **Appointment** (if the office requires it)

## At the DMV
- Vision test
- Knowledge test (if required for your case)
- Road test (if applicable)
- Pay fees and get temporary license

## Local notes
- Look up **wait times** and appointment availability for Durham/nearby offices.
- Morning slots are often less crowded.
